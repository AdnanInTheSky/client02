We are proud to announce that YCI Bangladesh is joining the 𝗡𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻𝗮𝗹 𝗦𝗲𝗺𝗶𝗻𝗮𝗿 𝗼𝗻 𝗖𝗹𝗶𝗺𝗮𝘁𝗲 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗗𝗶𝘀𝗮𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗿 𝗥𝗲𝘀𝗶𝗹𝗶𝗲𝗻𝗰𝗲 (𝗡𝗦𝗖𝗗𝗥) as a Sustainability Partner. This event is being organized by 𝗜𝗻𝘀𝘁𝗶𝘁𝘂𝘁𝗲 𝗼𝗳 𝗗𝗶𝘀𝗮𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗿 𝗠𝗮𝗻𝗮𝗴𝗲𝗺𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝗮𝗻𝗱 𝗩𝘂𝗹𝗻𝗲𝗿𝗮𝗯𝗶𝗹𝗶𝘁𝘆 𝘀𝘁𝘂𝗱𝗶𝗲𝘀 (𝗜𝗗𝗠𝗩𝗦), 𝗨𝗻𝗶𝘃𝗲𝗿𝘀𝗶𝘁𝘆 𝗼𝗳 𝗗𝗵𝗮𝗸𝗮, in association with Center for DMERT, Friendship, CDD, SAWAB, ASB and German cooperation. 

This national level seminar will bring together academics, researchers, policymakers, development practitioners, and youth leaders to engage in meaningful, evidence based discussions on climate change, disaster risk reduction, and resilience building in Bangladesh.

As a youth platform, we’re proud to be a part of this important discussion.
