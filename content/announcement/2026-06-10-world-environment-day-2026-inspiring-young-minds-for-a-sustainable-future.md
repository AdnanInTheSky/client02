The environment is not merely a resource we inherit, rather it is a responsibility we share. As climate change, pollution, biodiversity loss, and environmental degradation continue to challenge our world, the need for environmental awareness and youth engagement has never been greater.

On the occasion of *World Environment Day 2026, YCI Bangladesh* is delighted to organize a special program at *Panam City High School, Sonargaon,* bringing together students, educators, and young changemakers in a collective effort to promote environmental consciousness and sustainable action.

The event will feature an *Essay Competition, Public Speaking Competition, and Tree Plantation Program* , providing students with opportunities to express their ideas, strengthen their communication skills, and contribute directly to environmental conservation. Through these activities, we aim to encourage critical thinking, inspire responsible citizenship, and foster a deeper connection between young people and the environment.

📍 *Venue:* Panam City High School, Sonargaon

📅 *Date:* Thursday, June 11, 2026

We firmly believe that today's students are tomorrow's leaders. By empowering young people with knowledge, awareness, and a sense of responsibility, we can build a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable future for all.

Let us come together to celebrate World Environment Day not only through words, but through meaningful action.

🌿 *Be aware. Be responsible. Be the change.*
