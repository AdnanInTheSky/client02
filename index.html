Our Commitment to Bring Change

We envision a climate-resilient Bangladesh where youth-led innovation, inclusive community participation, and sustainable solutions create a greener, healthier, and more just future for all.

Our mission is to empower young people with knowledge, leadership skills, and resources to address climate challenges, uplift vulnerable communities, and promote sustainable development across Bangladesh.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is YCI Bangladesh?
YCI Bangladesh is a youth-led initiative working to strengthen climate resilience, environmental awareness, and sustainable development across Bangladesh through education, advocacy, and community-based action.
How can I volunteer with YCI?
Join our volunteer network by filling out the form on our Contact page. We welcome students, professionals, and community leaders passionate about climate action.
Do you work only in Dhaka?
No—we operate nationwide with active chapters in Chittagong, Sylhet, Khulna, and Rajshahi, focusing on locally relevant climate challenges.
